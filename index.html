About

Our Church

Our mission is to share the Love of Jesus Christ
through serving and caring.

We have a strong sense of community within our congregation where men, women, and children of all ages here are stretched to learn more about their own faith and role in the church. No matter your denomination or affiliation we invite you to our Sunday worship service.

Jack H Justus

Interim Pastor

Jack grew up in the central Indiana, and moved to Texas in 1992. He attended Johns Hopkins University and Towson University in Baltimore earning degrees in Engineering and Applied Physics. Jack surrendered to the ministry at Cedar Creek Baptist Church in Whitney TX in the summer of 2004, and was ordained on February 17th, 2013 at the Immanuel Fellowship Church in Galveston, TX. Jack has 5 Children ages 35, 30, 27, 23 and 19; and Jack has 2 grandchildren with 2 more on the way.
