Welcome To First Christian Church

What to Expect?

First Christian Church in Victoria, Texas is a welcoming congergation full of lovers of the Lord Jesus. As a first time visitor you'll be welcomed with a warm smile and a gift basket for spending your Sunday with us. Our services are traditional in style and include worship hymns, Bible readings, and a message from our pastor. For any specific questions regarding our services feel free to reach out to us.
